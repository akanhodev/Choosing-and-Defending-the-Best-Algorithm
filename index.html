5. Conclusion

✅ Recommended Algorithm: Greedy
The idea is simple: sort tasks by end time, then pick them one by one as long as they don't overlap with the previous one.

Brute Force explores every possible combination (2ⁿ). At n=30, that's over a billion cases. It's only useful to verify correctness of the greedy algorithm on small inputs in automated tests.
